## Question 1
**Which of the following types of investment risks are illustrated in John's situation with yacht-building securities that lost value due to a luxury tax?**
1. Market risk - systematic risk affecting all investments
2. Legislative risk - risk from changes in laws and regulations
3. Business risk - risk from company-specific operations
4. Interest rate risk - risk from changing interest rates

**A. 1 and 2**
**B. 2 only**
**C. 2 and 3**
**D. 3 and 4**

**Answer: B (2 only)**
**Explanation:** John's situation specifically demonstrates legislative risk (2) because the loss resulted from a congressionally imposed luxury tax. This is not market risk (1) which affects all investments, nor business risk (3) which is company-specific operational risk, nor interest rate risk (4) which relates to changes in borrowing costs.
