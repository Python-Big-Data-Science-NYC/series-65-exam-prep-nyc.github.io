### **Question 21-40: Quick Exam Structure Questions**

**21. Exam format:** Computer-based at PSI testing centers

**22. Question format:** 4 multiple choice options (A, B, C, D)

**23. "Select all that apply" questions:** NOT used on Series 65

**24. Scenario-based questions:** Many questions present client scenarios

**25. "Best answer" approach:** Sometimes multiple answers seem correct; choose the BEST one

**26. Exam frequency:** Can be taken any business day (subject to availability)

**27. Testing centers:** Located throughout U.S. and some international locations

**28. Personal items:** Lockers provided; no personal items in testing room

**29. Scratch paper:** Provided and collected (typically 2 sheets)

**30. Calculator:** Basic calculator provided on-screen (no personal calculators)

**31. Tutorial before exam:** 10-minute optional tutorial on computer use

**32. "Review screen" at end:** Shows all questions, flagged items, unanswered questions

**33. Exam language:** English only (no foreign language versions)

**34. Special accommodations:** Available with proper documentation (extra time, etc.)

**35. Passing score reported:** Immediately after exam completion

**36. Score report includes:** Pass/fail and performance by major topic area

**37. Retake waiting period:** 30 days if failed (can retake sooner with special permission)

**38. Maximum attempts per year:** Unlimited (subject to waiting periods)

**39. Exam validity:** Passing score doesn't expire, but must be used with registration

**40. Most tested topics:** Fiduciary duty, ethics, regulations, suitability

## Summary & Key Exam Information

### **Exam Structure:**
- **Total Questions:** 140 (130 scored + 10 pretest)
- **Time:** 180 minutes (3 hours)
- **Passing Score:** 72% (94/130 correct)
- **Question Format:** Multiple choice only (4 options)
- **Content Areas:** 
  - Section I: Economics & Analysis (15%)
  - Section II: Investment Vehicles (10%)
  - Section III: Client Recommendations (30%)
  - Section IV: Laws & Regulations (45%)

### **Registration Process:**
1. Firm files Form U4 through Web CRD
2. Receive eligibility email from PSI (1-3 days)
3. Schedule exam online or by phone
4. 120-day eligibility window
5. Bring 2 forms of ID to testing center
6. Results immediately after exam

### **Testing Accommodations:**
- Available for documented disabilities
- Submit request 30+ days before desired date
- Documentation from qualified professional
- Common accommodations: extra time, separate room, assistive technology
- PSI handles all accommodation requests

### **Scoring System:**
- Immediate pass/fail results
- Percentage score and topic performance
- Diagnostic report if failed
- 30-day waiting period before retake
- No penalty for guessing (but eliminate wrong answers first)
- Scores maintained indefinitely

### **Exam Day Tips:**
1. Arrive 30 minutes early
2. Bring required ID
3. Use provided scratch paper/calculator
4. Pace yourself (~1.4 min/question)
5. Flag uncertain questions
6. Answer every question (no penalty for wrong)
7. Review flagged questions if time permits

### **Preparation Recommendations:**
- Study time: 100-150 hours typical
- Use multiple study materials
- Take practice exams under timed conditions
- Focus on weak areas identified in practice tests
- Understand concepts, not just memorization
- Review regulations and ethics thoroughly
