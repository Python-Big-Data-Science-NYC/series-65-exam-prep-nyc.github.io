### **Question 14**
Which statement about the "integral part doctrine" is CORRECT?
A. It applies only to investment advisers
B. It allows unregistered individuals to perform clerical functions

**Correct Answer:** C. It requires registration if securities activities are regular part of business

**Explanation:** The integral part doctrine holds that if securities transactions are a regular, integral part of one's business, registration as a broker-dealer is required even if not the primary business.
