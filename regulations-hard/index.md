# Investment Adviser Regulation: 50 Complex Questions with Detailed Explanations

**1. Which of the following statements about brochure delivery are TRUE under state rules?**
I. Delivery must occur at least 48 hours before contract signing
II. If delivered at contract signing, client must have 5-business-day penalty-free termination right
III. Annual updates are required within 90 days of fiscal year end
IV. No delivery required if there are no material changes

A) I and II
B) I, II and III
C) II and IV
D) I, II and IV

**Correct Answer: A**

**Explanation:**
- **I is CORRECT**: State rules require delivery at least 48 hours before contract signing.
- **II is CORRECT**: If delivered at signing, client must have 5-business-day penalty-free termination right.
- **III is WRONG**: Annual updates are required within 120 days (not 90) of fiscal year end.
- **IV is WRONG**: Even with no material changes, some form of offer/notice may still be required in certain cases.

IV. Clients with over $1 million in assets

A) I and II
B) I, II and III
C) II and III
D) I, II, III and IV
